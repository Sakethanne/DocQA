Good excercise habits to develop
Here are 11 tips to improve your healthy excercise habits:

Wear appropriate clothing and footwear for your chosen activity.

Practice proper form and technique to maximize effectiveness and minimize injury risk.

Incorporate flexibility and mobility exercises into your routine to improve range of motion.

Mix up your routine to prevent boredom and plateaus.

Set realistic fitness goals.

Choose activities you enjoy to make exercise sustainable.

Start with a warm-up and end with a cool-down to prevent injuries.

Incorporate a variety of exercises to work different muscle groups.

Gradually increase intensity and duration to avoid overexertion.

Listen to your body and rest when needed.

Stay hydrated before, during, and after exercise.
